

            The Research, Information and Statistics Department

             The main functions of this Department are as follows:

          * conducting conceptual and applied research on the
            elaboration and implementation of monetary policy and
            analysing and forecasting macroeconomic trends and
            situations in financial markets;
          * preparing consolidated draft guidelines for uniform
            national monetary policy;
          * drafting the Central Bank's consolidated annual reports;
          * centralising statistical information flows;
          * compiling and publishing the Bank of Russia’s
            publications, including the Bank of Russia’s annual
            reports and bulletins of statistics and data analysis;
          * organising and maintaining the library and bibliographic
            services of the Bank of Russia.
